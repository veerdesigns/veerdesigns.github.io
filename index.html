    • What are Gemstones?  

      Gemstones are basically rocks or minerals that are cut and polished to enhance their natural beauty. They are of various colors and shapes. Human kind has always been fascinated by gemstones and their beauty. Today, gemstones are used for astronomical as well as ornamental purposes. The beautiful cut, shine, and shapes attract a lot of people from all over the world.

    • What is the history of gemstones?  

      Gemstones are basically mineral crystals which have dazzling brilliance and unique shape. They were found and unearthed many centuries ago and ever since that time, they have maintained their position in the hearts of human beings. It is believed that gemstones possess healing powers. In ancient times, Kings and Queens used to wear them as talismans and amulets that helped in protecting them from ghosts and evil. During those times, gemstones were used only by the rich and royal class.
      There are many types of gemstones available today. They are characterized as precious or semiprecious, organic or inorganic and natural or synthetic. Diamond, Ruby, Sapphire and Emerald are classified as Precious stones and all other gemstones are called semi precious stones. This classification was made during ancient times based on the rarity and quality of the gemstones.

    IIGL-GTL- Certification

    Every report issued by IGI-GTL, Delhi undergoes meticulous expert analysis of the stones and jewelry submitted for testing. This analysis is based on scientific tests conducted using state-of-the-art Gemmological equipment. Read More

    IGI LAB Certification

    IGI is the worlds largest certification lab for diamonds and colored gemstones, known for its unified international grading system and central governing body. Beyond the standard 4 Cs (Color, Clarity, Cut, and Carat Weight), IGI adds a C for Confidence, assuring consumers of the quality of their gems. Read More

    GRS LAB Certification

    Founded in 1996 by Dr. Adolf Peretti, Gem Research SwissLab (GRS) is a top independent gemmological lab. It specializes in identifying and analyzing high-quality gemstones, verifying their origins, and spotting imitations. With advanced technology and expert staff, GRS is highly regarded globally for gemstone treatment analysis and color grading. Read More

    GIA LAB Certification

    Founded in 1931, the Gemmological Institute of America (GIA) is a prominent non-profit that enhances gemmology and jewelry education. It establishes global standards with its "4 Cs" (Cut, Carat, Clarity and Colour) and International Diamond Grading System. Since 2004, GIA has contributed to the Indian gem industry with a cutting-edge lab in Mumbai. Read More
