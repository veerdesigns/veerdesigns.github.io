About KAP JEWELS

KAP JEWELS is your trusted partner in the world of natural gemstones. Located in the heart of Jaipur's famous Johri Bazar, we have been serving customers with 100% authentic and certified gemstones for years.

We specialize in high-quality Pink Sapphire, Yellow Sapphire (Pukhraj), Blue Sapphire (Neelam), Kunzite, and Ruby (Manik). Each gemstone in our collection is carefully selected, tested, and certified to ensure you receive only the finest quality.

Our commitment to authenticity, quality, and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred choice for gemstone enthusiasts, astrologers, and jewelry lovers across India.
