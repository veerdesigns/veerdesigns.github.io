Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Sarda Infotech offer?

Sarda Infotech is a full-service digital marketing agency. We offer social media marketing, SEO, Google and Facebook Ads, website and landing page development, performance marketing, content creation, influencer marketing consulting, and brand strategy.

How long does it take to see results?

Some results like social media engagement or ad reach are visible within weeks. SEO, branding, and long-term lead nurturing may take 2–3 months to show strong traction. We work with both short-term and long-term goals in mind.

What platforms do you work with?

We manage campaigns across Facebook, Instagram, Google, YouTube, LinkedIn, and WhatsApp. We also help brands build their online presence via websites, landing pages, SEO, and influencer channels.

How do you measure success?

We track KPIs like ROI, conversion rate, cost-per-lead, engagement rate, organic growth, and click-through rates — and tailor these metrics based on your business goals.
